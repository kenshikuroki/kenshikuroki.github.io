About Me

Hello! I am Kenshi Kuroki (黒木健志), a postdoctoral researcher in theoretical high-energy nuclear physics. After completing my Ph.D. in March 2024, I am currently conducting research at Institute of Modern Physics, Chinese Academy of Sciences. I am committed to continuously advancing my research capabilities and expanding my contributions to the field of nuclear physics.

Research Interests

Quantum Chromodynamics (QCD), Relativistic Hydrodynamics, High-Energy Nuclear Collisions, Hadron Interactions, Hadron Structures, etc.
